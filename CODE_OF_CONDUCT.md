# Code of Conduct

This project is for practical, research-oriented robotics software.
Contributions are expected to stay technical, respectful, and focused
on improving the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct about technical tradeoffs without personal attacks.
- Assume contributors may come from different robotics, AI, or software
  engineering backgrounds.
- Keep issues and pull requests scoped to reproducible behavior,
  concrete proposals, or clearly stated questions.
- Respect privacy around real robot logs, building maps, screenshots,
  credentials, and deployment details.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal insults, or discriminatory comments.
- Publishing another person's private information without consent.
- Repeated off-topic comments after a maintainer has asked to refocus.
- Abusive use of project communication channels.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or delete comments; close issues or pull
requests; block accounts; or otherwise limit participation when behavior
harms the project or its contributors.

To report a conduct issue, contact the maintainer through the repository
owner profile. Include links, screenshots, or other context when possible.
